The Best Movies According to Mihai

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

It suffered at the box office and failed to win any Oscars, but the 1994 prison drama is still seen by many as greater than The Godfather.

Shutter Island

A relentlessly chilling mystery and a white-knuckle thriller.

American Pie

It is hilarious from start to finish.
